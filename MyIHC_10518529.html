Way of Working MyIHC

MyIHC will work in sprints of 3 weeks starting in sprint 1

Every day there is daily stand up

Every week there is a refinement session of 1 hour

Every sprint will end with a retrospective

In sprint 1 and 2 there is a sprint review at the end for the team (stakeholders can be invited if wanted)

In sprint 3 there is a review session/demo for all stakeholders and team (if the release is ready)
